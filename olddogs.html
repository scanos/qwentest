Welcome to Old Seadogs FC

The Old Seadogs Football Club is a veteran team founded in 1995 for players who still have the passion for the beautiful game but may have lost a step or two over the years. We compete in local veteran leagues and tournaments, focusing on camaraderie, sportsmanship, and love of the game.

Our motto: "Still barking, still biting!"

About Old Seadogs

Founded in 1995 by a group of former professional and semi-professional players who weren't ready to hang up their boots, the Old Seadogs have been a fixture in the veteran football community for over 25 years.

We welcome players over 35 who still have the passion to compete. While we take our football seriously, we prioritize fun, friendship, and fair play above all else.

The team is named after our founder's beloved retired sailing dog, who would still bark excitedly at every ball but could no longer chase it with the same vigor - much like our players!
